## 🎼 Production tips
- **Voice:** CapCut TTS → a clear, neutral English "presenter" voice. Generate per-segment so timing is easy to nudge.
- **Music:** one subtle electronic/ambient track at ~10–15% volume; duck slightly under narration.
- **Captions:** turn on CapCut auto-captions from the TTS so it's accessible and readable on mute.
- **Pacing:** if narration runs long, it's fine to let visuals breathe — silence with music is better than rushed speech.
- **End on the logo** for at least 2–3 seconds.

Total narration ≈ 150–170 words across ~2:45 → comfortable, unhurried pace
